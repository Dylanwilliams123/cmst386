Describe how search engines work.

Search engines work by taking in data from keywords and crawling the internet in search of content that contains those keywords. These bots go through billions of webpages using data from those pages to find new URLs to add to their index. This information is stored so it can later be retrieved when the user searches for information that is contained on those web pages.

Explain the importance of keywords and descriptions as meta tags.

Keywords and descriptions as meta tags are very important for search engine optimization and accessibility. Having good keywords and meta data increases the chances of a user finding your site. This will lead to more traffic from users that have interest in your content, products, or services. The more specific your keywords and descriptions are the higher up your website will be ranked when searched.

List the four elements that comprise writing relevant content links.

The four elements that comprise writing relevant content links are technical SEO, Content, On-site SEO, and Off-site SEO. Search Engine Optimization is the most important part of writing relevant content. Making use of these four elements when writing your content will help get your website discovered.

What constitutes good heading hierarchy and include an example with more than one heading tag.

Good heading hierarchy is the way headings are structured on a page. The h1 tag is the largest heading and therefore will be at the top of the page. H2-H6 should cascade down naturally as they are needed. You should not skip levels when structuring your page. If you have your h1 heading and need a subheading you should use h2 and not skip to h3.

For Example:

This is the H1 Heading

This is the H2 Heading

This is the H3 Heading

This is the H4 Heading

This is the H5 Heading
This is the H6 Heading
