Explain the importance of responsive design and its impact on the target audience.

A website may adapt to any screen size using responsive design. It eliminates the need to create and manage two distinct versions of your website and is just as user-friendly on mobile devices as it is on tablet and desktop computers. This has an influence on a target audience since it takes less time, saves loading time, works well on diverse devices, and makes all sites always available.

Google Analytics provides a wealth of data for free to help assess the success of websites. Describe three different kinds of reports that will tell you what information your target audience is accessing .

You may focus your geographic targeting with the use of location reports. You may learn which towns, states, and nations are home to the people that visit your website. You can choose the right tone for your messaging by knowing if the majority of your website visitors are men or women. Knowing the gender and age of your target will help you write copy that will appeal to them more. Age reports are crucial because they let you decide which social media platforms to prioritize based on the average age of the visitors to your website. One of the greatest ways to determine where your audience spends the most time is based on age.
